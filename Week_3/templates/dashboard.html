Energy Analytics Dashboard

Key exploratory data insights extracted from the steel industry dataset. These insights reveal major electrical patterns, loading structures, and feature correlations that guide our predictive model.

1. Energy Consumption by Hour of Day

Line chart mapping the average energy consumption (kWh) over a 24-hour diurnal cycle. Notice the sharp increases in demand beginning around 08:00 and peaking during afternoon operational shifts before tapering off after 18:00.

2. Energy Consumption by Industry Load Type

Bar chart analyzing active energy consumption across the three designated system load levels (Light, Medium, Maximum). Average power usage transitions from a minor 2.92 kWh during Light Load phases up to a substantial 74.19 kWh during peak Maximum Load periods.

3. Feature Correlation Matrix

Correlation heatmap showing standard Pearson correlations between numerical electrical inputs. Note the strong positive correlation between active energy (Usage_kWh) and Reactive Power, CO2 levels, and lagging power factor parameters.
